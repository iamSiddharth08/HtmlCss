This is the receipe of making tea

The following are the ingredients to make an ideal tea

  • Milk
  • Water
  • Tea powder
  • Sugar
  • Spices, for taste

The following is the method to make an ideal tea

  1. Take water and warm till it starts boiling.
  2. After it starts boiling, add required tea powder and required sugar.
  3. After this add the required spices for the taste
  4. After the boiling water has the aroma of the tea powder, add milk into it.
  5. Now, boil it for a few minutes more...
  6. And that's it!!! Your ideal tea is ready to serve to you or you can directly have it.
